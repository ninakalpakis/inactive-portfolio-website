about me

My journey into tech started with a love for design and an eye for detail. As a graduate student in business, I decided to add some coding skills to my repertoire – just to keep in my back pocket. But what started as curiosity soon changed to passion, and it wasn't long before I was hooked. I loved the entire process of creating a project... from building the bones of a site with HTML, to meticulously curating its design with CSS, all while collaborating with others along the way.

Through learning to code, I realized I had found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my love for design and eye for detail to write clean and elegant code. I focus on writing organized code with HTML that provides efficient and effective communication, and I am passionate about creating clean designs with CSS, JavaScript, React, and responsive web design principles that are both beautiful and functional.
